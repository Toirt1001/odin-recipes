Banana Bread Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 rip bananas, mashed
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 TBsp milk

Instructions

  1. Start by heating the oven to 375F. Grease and flour a 15x10-inch jelly roll pan.
  2. Now the Brownies, in a large bowl, beat together the sugar, sour cream, butter, and eggs until creamy. Blend in the mashed bananas and vanilla extract.
  3. Then add the flour,baking soda, salt to the bowl. Blend everything for 1 minute. stir in the chopped walnuts.
  4. Spread the batter evenly into the prepared pan and bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until it turns golden brown.
  5. Meanwhile, for the frosting, heat 1/2 cup butter in a large saucepan over medium heat until it starts boiling. Let the butter turn a delicate brown then remove it from the heat immediately.
  6. Add the powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk to the browned butter. Whisk it all together untill smooth. The consistency should be thicker than a glaze but thinner than frosting.
  7. Using a spatula, spread the brown butter frosting over the warm bars. It's easier to spread the frosting while the bars are still warm.
