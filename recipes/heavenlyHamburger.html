Heavenly Hamburger

Ingredients

  • 2 lb. ground beef
  • 2 TBSP sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 30 ounces tomato sauce
  • 16 ounces egg noodles
  • 1 pint sour cream
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • onion powder
  • 2 cup Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef, then drain and add sugar, salt, garlic powder, and tomato sauce. let simmmer 10 minutes.
  2. Boil 16 Oz. egg noodles, drain, and add sour cream cheese and onion powder to taste.
  3. Layer 1/2 meat mixture, noodles, and then repeat
  4. sprinkle with 2 cups Mozzarella and 2 cups cheddar cheese . bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
